Bio

Jacob Priller is a multimedia producer. With over 9 years of experience in music and a passion for audio/music production software, Disc jockeying and video production, Jacob’s recent focus has been in live performance. An award winning elementary, middle, and high school band percussionist, Jacob’s love for music grew in Long Reach high school drumline. Subsequently, he was inspired to create his own music: hip hop, edm, dubstep, house, and dance. In addition he has garnered a following as a DJ , “DJ Pistolpete” after the basketball player pete maravich. Currently Jacob is pursuing his AA in Audio Video Media Production (AVMP). As a part of his studies Jacob created a sports news series called “the 2 minute drill” where he talks about the current news and drama in sports . Jacob has also produced and directed corporate videos for local realtors.
